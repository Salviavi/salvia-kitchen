Getting Started

Healthy living made simple in just 3 steps

01

Share your food loves (and hates)

Skip the stress of meal planning! Mindy Kitchen App crafts a fully personalized weekly menu to match your taste while keeping nutrition balanced. Tell us what you like and dislike. Dislike fish? Love spicy? Our smart system builds a custom meal plan that fits your preferences.

02

Approve Your Plan

Once a week, check your meal plan on the App and modify it however you like. You can swap meals, change ingredients, or even add your own favorites. Personalize your meals until your weekly plan feels perfect.

03

Right food, Right Time

Our top chefs prepare your chosen meals daily and deliver them at the time that suits you best, out of 3 options. Change your delivery slot anytime!
